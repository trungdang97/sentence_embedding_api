I. GENERAL INFOR
vnPara: A corpus for the paraphrase identification task in Vietnamese language
Authors: Ngo Xuan Bach (1), Tran Thi Oanh (2), Nguyen Trung Hai (1), Tu Minh Phuong (1)
(1) Department of Computer Science, Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology, Vietnamese
(2) International School, Vietnam National University, Hanoi
If you have any problems, please contact us at ngoxuanbachkcn@gmail.com

II. FORMAT
The corpus consists of 3083 sentence pairs associated with paraphrase labels
1: paraphrase
0: non-paraphrase
Sentences1.txt, Sentences2.txt: each file contains 3083 sentences (each sentence in one line) 
Labels: contains 3083 labels corresponding to 3083 sentence pairs
